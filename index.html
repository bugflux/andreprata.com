About me

I'm 26 years old. Portuguese, living in Lisbon.

I completed a Masters in Computer and Telematics Engineering at the University of Aveiro. I wrote my masters dissertation while holding a Junior Research position with the ATNoG group at Instituto de Telecomunicações.

After one year at Novabase, where I developed three independent projects for Vodafone Portugal, I opted to join a larger team, split across Paris and Lisbon in a project for BNP Paribas. Right now I'm a part of the delivery team at Feedzai.

During my spare time I like to hang out with my camera/friends, listen/play music, and go to the movies. I am particularly interested in photography, as well as psychology and sociology.
